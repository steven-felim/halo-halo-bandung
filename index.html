Bandung is the perfect destination for travelers who love nature and adventure. Surrounded by misty mountains, volcanic landscapes, refreshing waterfalls, and cool highland scenery, the city offers endless outdoor experiences.

Beyond its natural beauty, Bandung is also a haven for food lovers, famous for iconic local dishes such as batagor, seblak, and nasi timbel that reflect the richness of Sundanese culture.
